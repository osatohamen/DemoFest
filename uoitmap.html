OUR PURPOSE

Circe is the daughter of Son God Helios, whoâ€™s father exiled her into a deserted island for eternity. During her time on this island, Circe progressed her strength in her magic which we see as a symbolic reference of the transformative power of self discovery. Self-discovery is the process of gaining a deeper understanding of oneself, while exploring oneâ€™s inner world. Self-discovery in the modern day world can be seen as an individual who goes into therapy, mindfulness practices, introspection and journaling, and engaging in new events and experiences that can challenge one's credence. During the university experience, the life of students revolves around understanding not only their worldview but also gaining a deeper understanding of their true nature. In university, a student will transform into adulthood and experience the actuality of what real life has to offer.

Our project is dedicated to university students and giving them guidance through an easily accessible resource. This platform will be dedicated to specific functions that will focus solely on the wellness and mental health of the student. Features such as providing a calendar with all clubs activities and events, providing self assessment tools to better understand their mental health needs, financial resources such as budgeting, academic resources such as study tips, time management skills, better communications, study tips, anxiety and stress relief tips e.t.c.

The idea of the website is not to act as a therapist for students, but to create a safer space to alleviate pressure and reduce the stigma of receiving mental health help which can be caused by societal pressures and generational trauma. It is already known that our university has multiple different pages that students can go to but our goal with this website is to create a space where students from the university can chat with each other to provide guidance; while being able to access resources in a faster and easier way through one website.
